Accessible Parking

Accessible Parking is an application which leverages the Google Maps API to plot the locations of disabled parking spaces in the Greater Sydney Metropolitan area.

The objective of the application is to reduce the complexity in locating available and affordable disabled parking.

This is an ongoing project and I am currently in the process of developing functionality for users to plot, upload and share their own locations of disabled parking spaces.

Rescue Cat Matcher

A Ruby terminal app to match rescue cats across different shelters in NSW to members of the public who want to adopt a cat.

The app gathers information from the user and ensures that cats are matched to suitable owners based on a set of mandatory criteria.

The user is allowed to choose a cat from a range of cats which have been matched to their criteria.
